The Methodology
1. Exposure: Review the batch of 8 items. Don't memorize, just familiarize.
2. Recall: Test yourself. We force 2-way recall (Symbol → Meaning AND Meaning → Symbol) to ensure mastery.
3. Loop: The system remembers what you missed. You will loop through ONLY the missed items until you get them all right.
